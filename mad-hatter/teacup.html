The Hatter's Second Riddle:

"I sleep through the party, I dream through the day,
My home is with letters that march in array.
Twenty-three, fifteen, fourteen, four, five, eighteen
Will show you the path that I call my demesne."

The numbers spell out letters (A=1, B=2, C=3...)
